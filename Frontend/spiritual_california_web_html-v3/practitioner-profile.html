Update
Just returned from a week of silence at a small hermitage in the Santa Cruz mountains. No phone, no screens — just breath, birdsong, and the slow unfolding of what's been waiting beneath the noise. I'm back with new sessions available in March. If you've been feeling the pull toward stillness, this might be your moment. 🌸

I came to Ayurveda the way most people do — through exhaustion. After years of working in tech in San Francisco, I found myself in a state of burnout so complete that I couldn't remember what it felt like to feel well. A friend recommended an Ayurvedic practitioner in Marin, and that first consultation changed the direction of my life.

I trained for three years at the California College of Ayurveda, completed an advanced practicum in Kerala, India, and studied Yoga Nidra and Tibetan breathwork practices with teachers in Dharamsala. I am certified by the National Ayurvedic Medical Association (NAMA) and have been in private practice in Los Angeles since 2020.

My approach is deeply individualized. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all wellness. I work with your unique constitution, your life circumstances, and the specific challenges you're facing right now — whether that's burnout, digestive issues, sleep problems, anxiety, or a more diffuse sense that something is off and you can't quite name it.

I offer sessions in English and Russian, both online and in-person in Los Angeles. I also lead small group retreats and contribute to Soul Travels programs in India and Sri Lanka.
